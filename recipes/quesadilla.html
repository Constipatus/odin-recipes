Chicken Stuffed Quesadilla Recipe

  • 20 frozen Buffalo Style Chicken Breast Tenders
  • 4 flour tortillas (8 inch)
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar or Monterey Jack cheese (2 cups = 8 oz)
  • No-Stick Cooking Spray
  • Blue cheese dressing, optional
  1. Prepare chicken tenders according to package directions.
  2. Sprinkle half of 1 tortilla with 1/4 cup cheese. Top with 5 hot tenders and another 1/4 cup cheese. Fold tortilla in half over filling. Repeat with remaining ingredients.
  3. Spray large skillet with cooking spray; heat over medium heat. Add each quesadilla and cook about 1 minute per side or until tortilla is golden brown and cheese melts.
  4. Cut quesadillas into wedges. Serve with blue cheese dressing, if desired.
